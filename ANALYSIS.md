# Recyglo Website Analysis

## Overview
Recyglo is a waste management and ESG data analytics platform serving businesses across Asia-Pacific. The website showcases their services, company values, and impact stories.

## Key Sections Identified

### 1. Navigation & Header
- Logo (Recyglo)
- Main Navigation: Home, About Us, Our Solutions, Resources, Articles, Platforms
- Language Selector (ENG)
- CTA Button: "Contact Us"
- Secondary CTA: "Calculate Carbon Footprint"

### 2. Hero Section
- Large background image (green nature/sustainability themed)
- Main headline: "Welcome to RecyGlo"
- Subheading: "Empowering businesses in Myanmar, Vietnam, Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, and Korea with circular economy strategies and ISO-compliant reporting."
- Service process diagram (circular flow showing waste collection → sorting → recycling → reporting)
- Two CTA buttons: "Calculate Carbon Footprint" and "Our Solutions"

### 3. Testimonials Section
- Carousel/slider showing client testimonials
- Quotes from organizations like French Embassy, Trattoria Delina employees
- 1/5 indicator showing multiple testimonials

### 4. Services Section
- 6 main service cards:
  1. Waste Management Solutions
  2. Waste Auditing
  3. Reporting and Compliance
  4. Consulting and Training
  5. ESG Data Analytics
  6. Circular Economy

### 5. Company Values Section
- Mission: "Foster a more sustainable future through innovative waste, energy, and carbon management and ESG data analytics solutions"
- Vision: "Leading provider of zero-waste and zero-carbon energy efficient management solutions across Asia Pacific"
- Goal: "Implement ESG-driven waste management systems throughout Asia Pacific"
- Focus areas: Sustainability, Circular Economy, Waste & Energy Management, Green Financing, Smart Agriculture

### 6. Impact Section
- Team photos and gallery
- "RecyGlo Team Thailand" section with image gallery
- "See Gallery" CTA

### 7. Blog/Resources Section
- Featured blog posts with categories:
  - "RecyGlo Publishes Report on Thailand's Battle With Climate Change" (Sustainability)
  - "Thailand's Sustainable Future: The Significance of Renewable Energy" (Circular Economy, News)
  - "Understanding the importance of circular economy in Thailand" (Circular Economy)
- "See All" link to articles page

### 8. Footer
- Sections: Information, Services, Location, Opening Hours, Quick Contact
- Links to all main pages
- Service listings
- Operating locations: Thailand, Vietnam, Myanmar, Indonesia, South Korea, Singapore, Malaysia
- Hours: Monday to Friday 8:30 - 17:30
- Contact: Contact@recyglo.com, (+66)81 412 6842
- Social: @RecyGlo

## Design Characteristics
- **Color Scheme**: Green (sustainability), white, dark text
- **Typography**: Modern, clean sans-serif fonts
- **Imagery**: Nature/sustainability themed backgrounds, team photos, service diagrams
- **Layout**: Full-width sections, centered content, card-based components
- **Interactive Elements**: Carousels, hover effects, CTAs throughout
